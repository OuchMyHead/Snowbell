claire

You’re a great big sis and friend, an animal-lover and a devout Queen fan! We all see how dedicated you are to your work, family (especially Jack, Coco, Biscuit and the Fishies!) and to your many friends. You’re generous, fun and just a little bit “batty” (HeHeHe) but we wouldn’t change you (much) ☺ We’re so thrilled for you and Col – you’re such a great couple – he can cook and you can clean!

We can just see you both in 50 years, surrounded by a menagerie of animals in the house that Col built… We all hope that you and Col have a fantastic life together and that there will be plenty more surprises, laughs and adventures along the way.

Love to you both,
Kate, Kim and Liz & The "One-Two, One-Two, Testing", Pub Quiz Team (Lindsay, Guy, Lee, John and Ad)

P.S. After a few hours in the pub we decided to write you a little poem and believe it or not this was our best attempt… (it was originally to be read to the tune of Mary Mary Quite Contrary (as if you couldn’t have guessed!) but that may have gone awry at some point…)

Poem 2

Oh Clairey Clairey, quite contrary How does your garden grow, With Colin Bell and pets galore And big holes in the lawn (from the weed napalm incident…)

Known to many as “Snowy”, “the Bat Lady” or “Clairey-Peary” But to us a kind, caring, generous, music loving, bookworm, cinematic enthusiast and all around great girl!

So, here’s to your menagerie Wishing you all the happiness the would could bring on this special day.
